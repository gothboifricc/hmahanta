Insights

Listed below are the key insights I was able to gather from the dashboard and thus meet the aforementioned objectives:


ðŸ”¹Insight 1: Women are more likely to buy compared to men (women are ~65% more likely to buy).


ðŸ”¹Insight 2: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, and Tamil Nadu are the top 5 states (these 5 states together account for ~11 mil. in total sales.)


ðŸ”¹Insight 3: Adults (30-49 years) especially women of this age group(account for ~35% of all orders) are the greatest contributors to number of orders placed.


ðŸ”¹Insight 4: Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra channels have the maximum contributions to number of orders (together they account for ~80%).


Suggested Actions

Here are the actions I would suggest based on the insights I was able to collect from this dataset:


1. Prioritise targeting women customers of 30-49 years of age living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.


2. Prioritise focus on Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels. This can be done through ads/offers/coupons/promotional events etc.
